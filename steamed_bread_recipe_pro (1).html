SteamedBreadRecipe

Easy Guide to making the perfect steamed bread

Ingredients

  • 5 Cups of Cake wheat flour
  • 8 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 500ml warm water
  • 1 sachet instant yeast
  • 3 teaspoons cooking oil
  • 50g butter/margarine

Instructions

  1. Pour 5 cups of flour into an open bowl
  2. Add 8 teaspoons of sugar into the flour
  3. Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt into the flour & sugar mixture
  4. Add one packet of instant yeast into the mixture
  5. Melt 50g of butter or margarine and pour it into the flour mixture
  6. Add 500ml warm water into the dry mixture and mix instantly
  7. Knead the dough until fully combined
  8. Add 3 teaspoons of oil and knead the dough until it's non-sticky
  9. Apply oil on the bowl and put the dough back
  10. Cover the dough and put it in a warm environment for an hour and let it rise
  11. After letting the dough rise, cut it into small sections
  12. Apply oil or margarine inside some cups and then transfer the dough into the cups to fit
  13. Pour water inside a pot and let it boil for 5 minutes
  14. Put the cups with dough inside the pot with water (ensure that the water doesn't go inside the dough) and steam for about 40-45 minutes
  15. Serve and enjoy
